Good Times Dark Times A Return to Good Times

Luckily there are many others who are as nostalgic as I am so they have created ways to reach back into the past and play the games that I wasted my nights away playing. There is a humongous community of makers and builders that have created emulators that allow you to play old games on your own computer. However I find that using my keyboard just isn't the same a it used to be. Luckily there is a way to use the old style controllers as well.

Enter Raspberry Pi and Retropie

You can buy a Raspberry Pi for around $35, and a set of retro bluetooth controllers for around $50. Then with a little tinkering and youtubing you are ready to play all, and I mean ALL of the games that could be played on the old consoles on your big screen.

Someday when I have a little more cash to burn I will probably begin scouring the internet to find a second Fire Red GameBoy Advanced SP and the great games to go with so I can fully relive my childhood experience, but for now ROMS will get me through.
