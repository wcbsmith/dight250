While industrious can apply to a large industrial factory industrious can also apply to people who like to take matters into their own hands. This is a different type of industriousness but it is just as important to society.

Interestingly enough there, even though more and more of the things we need in life can be purchased from a store, there has been a semi-recent push to learn to build things for oneself. Or to create things that have never been done before. This is another example of those things that are industrious.
