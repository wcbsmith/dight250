Food Woodworking

The work of a craftsman comes in many forms and can be appreciated in many different ways. Even in the different "disciplines" that are available each master has their own take on craftsmanship and they make it their own.

This website is devoted to the idea of those craftsmen in discussing some of the disciplines in which they can be found.
